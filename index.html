Full Stack Web Developer

Welcome, my name's Austin here is some info about me and my coding experience.


  • A bit about me
    My background previously before going through the Rutger's coding boot camp was in project management, as a desire to go from simply managing and directing the outset of a project, I craved to be more hands on.
  • Why I code
    I have worked with building data bases in the past and have always enjoyed working with data and building out processes. I found myself at a point where I was struggling to get excel and access to do what I needed and display it in a visually appealing and intuitive way. To that end I realized I wanted to be able to truly be able to build out process and decided to pursue web development.
  • My passions
    Like I said I love working with data and found my passion working with the back-end of web development. In a way I knew I would gravitate towards back-end web dev due to the fact I always ended up working on refining processes and ensuring efficiency both at the out set and down the line.
